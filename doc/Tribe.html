Class: Tribe

Tribe

new Tribe(config)

A tribe is an automated agent that lives on a two-dimensional map Its population can grow or shrink depending on the conditions. It knows how to draw itself, but most importantly it can decide on an action every tick. If certain conditions are met, the tribe may decide to #colonize a neighboring tile. Otherwise, the tribe may decide to #move to a better spot. A random move may be taken if the tribe 'panics', meaning its #temperature goes over a certain limit. The temperature is reset after an action is performed. How long it takes before the tribe panics depends on its #patience.
